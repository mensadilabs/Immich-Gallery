Immich Gallery

Your Photos, Beautiful on Apple TV

A native Apple TV app for browsing your self-hosted Immich photo library with a stunning, TV-optimized interface.

✨ Key Features

📷

All Photos Tab

Browse your entire photo library in a beautiful grid layout with infinite scrolling and smooth navigation.

👥

People Tab

Discover photos organized by people with face recognition and animated thumbnail previews.

📁

Albums Tab with Shared Albums

Access your personal albums and shared albums with collaborative photo collections.

🔍

Smart Search

Intelligent search functionality to quickly find photos by content, location, or metadata.

🏷️

Tags View

Organize and browse photos by custom tags with dynamic animated thumbnails.

📺

Top Shelf Integration

Beautiful Apple TV Top Shelf integration showcasing your latest photos on the home screen.

⚙️

Complete Customization

Extensive customization options for interface, slideshow settings, and user preferences.

🎬

Slideshow Mode

Immersive full-screen slideshow with customizable timing and optional clock overlay.

👤

Multi User Support

Support for multiple Immich accounts with seamless switching between different servers.
